About Me

Hey, my name is Berengere, but please call me B!

My love for photography started at a very young age. First taking photos of my pets, my family, and everything really...Then I discovered my second passion :

traveling. This love for discovering new places and cultures brought me closer to people from all over the world.

Originally from France, I travelled the world for a year, I also lived in England, in Australia, and now in Canada.

I am passionate about immortalizing singular moments and catching your emotions through my lenses. I am an adept of candid shots and love how photography can create such a strong bond between people.
